7 Results and next steps

7.1 Results so far

  • We have a framework to respond to hurricanes:
    • input: mandatory and voluntary orders
    • output: produce rough predictions of evacuations and importations
  • From hypothetical scenarios, as of end Aug-early Sept, the risk of covid importations due to hurricane evacuations remains low

7.2 Next Steps

  • We have layout a mechanism to estimate the impact of importations in the reception counties spread
  • We will explore safegraph data to understand importations outside larger cities
  • Report, dashboard, publication
