4 Evacuations and receptions estimations

mandatory evacuations

  • \(h\) = hurrican hazard metric
  • \(h*([15\%,30\%])\) if \(h>0\)
  • 1% if low lying and \(h = 0\)

voluntary evacuations

  • \(h*([1\%,3\%])\) if \(h>0\)
  • 0% if \(h = 0\)

reception center proportion

  • 40% of total evacuations
  • equal preference for all reception counties
